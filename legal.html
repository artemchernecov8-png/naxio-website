Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · NAXIO Agency

We use minimal cookies. No tracking, no advertising cookies.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They help remember your preferences between visits.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These are required for the website to function:

  • naxio_consent — remembers your cookie consent choice. Expires in 365 days.
  • naxio_lang — remembers your language preference. Expires in 365 days.

What We Do NOT Use

  • Google Analytics or any analytics tracking
  • Facebook Pixel or social media trackers
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Third-party marketing cookies

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality (language preference won't be remembered).

To clear cookies: go to your browser Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cookies.
